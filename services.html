What We Do

Three services, one precision standard.

From laser cutting to surface coating to full fabrication — every service follows the same quality-first approach.

01

Laser Cutting Service

High-power CNC fiber laser cutting for precision patterns, intricate designs, and clean edges. We handle everything from thin sheet to heavy-gauge metal.

  • Stainless Steel, MS, Brass, Aluminium
  • MDF & Corten Steel
  • Custom patterns from your design files
  • Heavy-gauge capability
  • Millimetre precision tolerance
Request Quote
02

Coating Service (PVD, PU)

Professional surface treatment to enhance durability, colour, and finish quality. Available in a wide range of colours and textures.

  • PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coating
  • PU (Polyurethane) coating
  • Gold, Rose Gold, Black, Chrome tones
  • Anti-fingerprint finish available
  • Corrosion-resistant coating
Request Quote
03

Fabrication Services

Complete metal fabrication — with or without our cutting service. Welding, bending, assembly and installation for turnkey delivery.

  • With Fabrication (cut + build)
  • Without Fabrication (cut only)
  • Welding & assembly
  • On-site installation available
  • Gujarat & nationwide delivery
