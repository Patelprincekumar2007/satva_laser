About Satva Laser

Seven years of cutting metal into art.

From a single CNC machine in Gota to a trusted name among architects, interior designers and fabricators across Gujarat.

Our Story

Quality over quantity, on purpose.

Satva Laser is a trusted laser cutting factory in Ahmedabad, specializing in precision cutting for MS, SS, metal steel, aluminium, MDF, and Corten steel. Located in Gota, Ahmedabad, we serve architects, interior designers, fabricators, and businesses.

We offer designer metal panels, safety grills, MDF decorative panels, custom artwork, and architectural laser cut solutions with clean finishing, accurate execution, timely delivery, and value driven pricing.

Our commitment to quality over volume means every piece receives the attention it deserves — from pattern design to final finishing. We handle heavy-gauge metal thickness that most workshops turn away.

Materials We Work With

Six metals, one standard.

Every material we cut is chosen for the project — not the other way round.

Stainless Steel

Our most popular material. Corrosion-resistant, elegant — ideal for partitions, murals and fittings.

Mild Steel (MS)

Strong, affordable, versatile. Perfect for safety grills, heavy-duty screens and industrial applications.

Brass

Warm golden tones for luxury hardware — door handles, name plates and premium decorative pieces.

Aluminium

Lightweight and modern. Great for signage, decorative panels and outdoor applications.

MDF

Medium-density fibreboard for interior decorative panels — affordable, lightweight, design-forward.

Corten Steel

Weathering steel with rustic patina. Perfect for garden screens and outdoor architectural features.

Our Journey

Built one cut at a time.

THE BEGINNING

One Machine, One Vision

Started with a single CNC laser cutter in Gota, Ahmedabad — driven by the belief that precision metalwork could be both functional and beautiful.

GROWING

Architect Collaborations

Word spread through the architect community. Began receiving commissions for hotel lobbies, restaurant interiors, and luxury residences across Gujarat.

EXPANDING

In-House Product Line

Launched our own range — bollard lamps, murals, door handles, wall clocks — bringing design and manufacturing under one roof.

TODAY

7+ Years of Precision

A trusted name for over seven years. Serving architects, interior designers, fabricators and homeowners — with the same commitment to quality that started it all.
