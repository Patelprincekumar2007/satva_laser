// The Steel Itself

Two finishes, one steel.

Every jaali, mural and fitting leaves in one of two finishes — chosen for the room, not the other way round.

Matte

A smooth, non-reflective surface achieved through specialised treatment. Modern and elegant, it reduces glare and hides fingerprints — ideal for architectural, decorative and high-touch applications.

Matte Finish

Glossy

A high-shine, reflective surface that enhances stainless steel's natural character. Sleek and polished, it adds brightness and sophistication — perfect for decorative, upscale interiors.

Glossy Finish

// The Studio

Quality over quantity, on purpose.

With over seven years of expertise, Satva Laser has built a name as a trusted leader in laser cutting — known for prioritising quality over volume. Equipped with advanced machinery, we handle heavy metal thickness most workshops turn away, and deliver precision results others can't.

Our portfolio runs through collaborations with hoteliers, renowned architects and artists, alongside an exclusive in-house range — bollard lamps, murals, luxury door handles, wall clocks and more — blending innovation with elegance from a single studio floor in Gota, Ahmedabad.
